About

Built by a founder who's been in the room.

Clearline Advisory is led by Charlotte L. Rasmussen, an operations strategist with deep experience in scaling businesses from the inside.

With a background in global agency operations at one of the world's largest marketing networks, Charlotte has spent years building the systems that keep complex, multi-stakeholder organizations running smoothly — project management frameworks, cross-functional workflows, resource planning, and operational reporting.

She founded Clearline Advisory with a specific mission: to bring that same calibre of operational thinking to founder-led businesses that are ready to scale but don't yet need (or can't yet afford) a full-time COO.

Based in Toronto. Working with ambitious founders across the GTA.
